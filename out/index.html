About

I am a PhD student at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Xinbo Gao and Associate Prof. Jiaxu Leng. My research focuses on Video Anomaly Understanding (VAU) and embodied intelligence, aiming to build practical multimodal perception systems for challenging real-world scenarios.
