I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Technology at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. **[Xinbo Gao (高新波)](https://web.xidian.edu.cn/xbgao/)** and Associate Prof. **[Jiaxu Leng (冷佳旭)](https://faculty.cqupt.edu.cn/lengjiaxu/zh_CN/index.htm)**. My current research focuses on **Agentic Anomaly Understanding**, with interests in video anomaly understanding, active visual reasoning, and multimodal large language models.

My recent work studies how vision-language agents can actively acquire evidence, reason over anomalous events, and understand challenging real-world scenarios such as aerial anomalies, low-light surveillance, and autonomous-driving corner cases.
